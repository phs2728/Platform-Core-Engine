# Customer Interview Plan — PVP-001 Phase 12

> **Phase**: 12 of 14. Qualitative evidence for hypothesis validation.

---

## 1. Interview objectives

1. Validate or falsify persona assumptions.
2. Surface information that analytics cannot capture (motivation, hesitation, social context).
3. Verify Trust Stage mapping per persona.
4. Inform Hypothesis ID 001–004.

---

## 2. Sample plan

| Persona | Target | Recruiter | Time period |
|---|---|---|---|
| P1 Solo (5) | Booking.com + Hostelworld reviewers who stayed in Tbilisi | Marketing | Weeks 1–4 |
| P2 Couple (5) | Same source, filter by couple | Marketing | Weeks 1–4 |
| P3 Group (3) | Reddit / Facebook Tbilisi travel groups | Marketing | Weeks 2–5 |
| P4 Long-stay (3) | NomadList / Tbilisi digital-nomad community | Marketing | Weeks 2–6 |
| P5 Family (2) | Parent travel Facebook groups | Marketing | Weeks 3–6 |

Total: 18 interviews across 5 personas.

---

## 3. Interview structure (45 min)

| Time | Section |
|---|---|
| 0–5 | Warm-up (where they're from, why Tbilisi) |
| 5–15 | Booking flow walk-through (think-aloud on a competitor site + our proposed site) |
| 15–25 | Trust stage probing: what made them anxious, what made them confident, what made them hesitate? |
| 25–35 | Hypothesis-specific probes (gallery, sections, CTA, trust evidence) |
| 35–45 | Open-ended feedback |

---

## 4. Recruitment channels

- Booking.com post-stay survey outreach
- Hostelworld post-stay outreach
- Tbilisi travel Facebook groups (Tbilisi Travel Tips, Korea Travel to Georgia, etc.)
- NomadList (digital nomads in Tbilisi)
- Reddit r/Tbilisi, r/Georgia, r/SoloTravel

---

## 5. Compensation

- 30 GEL gift card (Tbilisi avg meal cost) per interview
- 50 GEL for long-stay (45 min, harder recruitment)
- Recorded only with explicit consent

---

## 6. Output format

Each interview produces:

1. Audio recording (if consented)
2. Transcript (Korean + English where applicable)
3. Empathy-map update
4. Hypothesis advance/reject note (if relevant evidence emerged)
5. Verbatim quotes file (for Stage-2 messaging + A/B test copy ideation)

---

## 7. Acceptance criteria

- [ ] ≥ 5 interviews per primary persona (P1, P2)
- [ ] ≥ 2 interviews per secondary persona (P3, P4, P5)
- [ ] All transcripts anonymized before storage
- [ ] Hypothesis advance/reject notes recorded per Hypothesis Lifecycle Standard
- [ ] At least one new (post-Alpha) hypothesis surfaced for Phase 14 evaluation (target outcome)

---

## 8. Risk register

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Recruitment shortfall | Pre-schedule 30% over target; pay premium on referral |
| Recording consent | Brief in advance; offer 20% extra compensation for recording consent |
| Cultural translation gaps | Use bilingual moderator for ko + ka speakers |
