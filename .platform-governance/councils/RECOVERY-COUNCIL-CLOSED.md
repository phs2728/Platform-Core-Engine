## 1. Closing statement

The Recovery Council is hereby closed. Its mandate — to recover the original Experience Engine source code — is **discharged without classification as RECOVERED or UNRECOVERABLE**. This is by design, per PCR-001 §1 item 3:

> Do not attempt further recovery.
> Do not classify it as recovered.
> Do not classify it as unrecoverable.

The Recovery Council's final disposition of the Experience Engine is the classification **UNKNOWN PROVENANCE**, filed at:

- `Experience_Recovery_Classification.md` (canonical classification)
- `.platform-governance/exceptions/EXC-EXPERIENCE-001.md` (registered exception)

## 3. Future Recovery Council activity

**No further Recovery Council missions will be issued under any circumstance.** This is the policy stated by Charter §1 ("No reconstruction from memory") and reinforced by PCR-001 §1 item 3. Any future attempt to recover lost Platform source code MUST first obtain a new Council Resolution superseding or amending PCR-001.

## 4. Successor Council

The Product Validation Council replaces the Recovery Council for any future Platform-source-disposition decisions. The Product Validation Council's mandate is recorded at `.platform-governance/councils/PRODUCT-VALIDATION-COUNCIL.md`.
