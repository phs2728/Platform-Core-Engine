# Project Brief — Envoy Hostel & Tours Website Remodel

**Date**: 15 July 2026
**From**: Platform Agency
**To**: Envoy Hostel & Tours (Tbilisi)
**Re**: Website Remodel — Premium Agency Engagement

---

## 1. What we're delivering

A premium agency-grade website for Envoy Hostel & Tours in Tbilisi, designed to convert direct bookings, project warmth and trust, and support your team's daily operations.

Specifically:

- A **public website** (Landing → Room Details → Booking → Confirmation flow) that travelers can use on phones and desktops in English, Korean, and Georgian.
- A **secure booking flow** connected to your existing payment provider (or a recommended one if you'd prefer).
- A **content management system (CMS)** so your team can update rooms, prices, photos, FAQs, and policies without writing code.
- An **AI chatbot** for common traveler questions (check-in time, luggage storage, neighborhood tips).
- An **admin dashboard** for reservations, payments, refunds, and guest communication.
- **Search engine optimization** so travelers on Google find you before they find OTAs.
- **Accessibility** (WCAG 2.1 AA) so all travelers, including those using assistive technology, can use the site.
- **Performance** targets (sub-2.5s Largest Contentful Paint on mobile) so travelers don't bounce.
- **Quality assurance** including a multi-stage review before launch.

---

## 2. What we're not delivering (in this scope)

- We are not redesigning your logo or your brand identity (we'll work within your existing Brand DNA / visual language).
- We are not rebuilding your property-management system (PMS). We integrate with your existing PMS through your booking engine's API.
- We are not creating a native iOS / Android app (the website is mobile-first and PWA-ready; you can launch an app later if desired).
- We are not running paid advertising (we'll set up analytics + SEO foundations; ad campaigns are a separate engagement).
- We are not translating content into languages other than English, Korean, and Georgian (additional languages can be added later).

---

## 3. Design direction (high-level)

The website will feel **warm, premium, and quietly confident**. Inspiration: editorial travel magazines, Aman-resort-grade restraint, Kinfolk-style spaciousness.

Visual principles:

- Big photography (your property, your neighborhood, your food).
- Generous white space (so the photos do the talking).
- Restrained typography (large titles, easy-to-read body).
- Trust signals placed where decisions are made (manager's photo near "Book now"; reviews and security badges near the payment step).
- A palette rooted in your hospitality brand (warm whites, deep wine accent, deep stone neutrals).

We will share three full-screen **design comps** (Landing, Room Detail, Booking step 3 — payment) for your review and feedback before coding begins.
