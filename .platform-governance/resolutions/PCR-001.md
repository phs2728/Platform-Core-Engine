```
Experience Engine Charter

The Experience Engine shall never evolve
through reconstruction.

It shall evolve only through
validated product experience.

Experience is a product-derived capability,
not a document-derived capability.

Every reusable Experience pattern
must originate from
Discovery Beta,
validated experiments,
and production evidence.

The Envoy project is designated as the
canonical reference implementation
for Experience Engine v2.

No reconstruction from memory.

No speculative implementation.

Reality first.

Customers first.

Platform second.
```

## 5. Operating interpretation (non-normative)

This section is **not** part of the Resolution text. It records operating guidance for execution.

### 5.1 Per-item implementation status (required actions)

| Item | Action | Status |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Freeze Platform Core v1.0 | To be executed by `git tag` stamping on `feature/platform-freeze-v1` HEAD, after this Resolution and the Freeze Manifest are committed |
| 2 | Register Experience Engine as Known Exception (UNKNOWN PROVENANCE) | Filed at `.platform-governance/exceptions/EXC-EXPERIENCE-001.md` (see §6.1) |
| 3 | No further recovery attempt; no RECOVERED claim; no UNRECOVERABLE claim | Honored by the Provenance Audit; see `Experience_Recovery_Classification.md` |
| 4 | Do not merge Experience working tree into Platform Core | Honored; `feature/experience-engine-rc1` remains separate |
| 5 | Begin Envoy MVP | Authorized; see §5.2 |
| 6 | Envoy = canonical Experience Engine validation project | Honored by Charter §2.2 |
| 7 | Only Beta-Experiment-EPS-validated capabilities become Experience v2 | Honored by Charter §1 |
| 8 | Platform knowledge evolves from production, never from reconstruction | Honored by Charter §1 |
| 9 | Close Recovery Council; open Product Validation Council | See §6.2 and §6.3 |

### 5.2 Envoy authorization (per items 5–6)

Envoy MVP is authorized. Envoy is hereby designated the **canonical Experience Engine validation project**. The Envoy `Discovery Alpha Framework` already in place at `/opt/data/Discovery_Alpha_Framework.md` is the Alpha entry point. Envoy `Discovery Beta` will be the Gate that validates any reusable capability destined for Experience Engine v2.

### 5.3 Council transitions (per item 9)

- **Recovery Council** is hereby CLOSED. The Recovery Failure Report filed at `Recovery_Failure_Report.md` is preserved as historical evidence. No further recovery missions will be issued under that Council.
- **Product Validation Council** is hereby OPENED. Its mandate:
  - Maintain the Evidence Promotion Standard (`/opt/data/Evidence_Promotion_Standard.md`).
  - Maintain the Experiment Registry (`/opt/data/Experiment_Registry_Specification.md`).
  - Validate reusable Envoy capabilities per Discovery Beta → Experiment → Evidence → Knowledge Candidate.
  - Promote validated patterns through Promotion Standards to Platform knowledge **only** with explicit owner approval and versioned rollback references.
